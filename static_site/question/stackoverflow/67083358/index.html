The $.fn.dataTable.moment plug-in can be used multiple times in one table, to accommodate different formats.

If I un-comment both your formatters at the same time, then your demo works OK.

Oh my gosh you're right! Wow. I guess sometimes the most apparent solution is the best one haha - snucky 14 Apr 2021 at 14:08
It's a nice plug-in. It makes use of DataTables' built-in type detection, behind the scenes, to check a column's data against each format. That's what lets it handle multiple formats (as long as the data in each column is consistent, of course). Then, moment.js does the rest. - andrewJames 14 Apr 2021 at 21:23
